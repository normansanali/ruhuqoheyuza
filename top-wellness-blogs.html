Living a life of wellness involves many aspects. The most obvious dimensions of wellness include diet and exercise. Regular health screenings are also essential for detecting potential health issues. While you may not be able to do them all at once, the right balance of these dimensions will help you achieve your goal of living a healthy, long-lasting life.
