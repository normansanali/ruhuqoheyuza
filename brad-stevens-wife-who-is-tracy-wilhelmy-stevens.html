Brad Stevens Wife: Who Is Tracy Wilhelmy Stevens?

August 03, 2024
blog

Tracy Wilhelmy Stevens is the wife of Brad Stevens whom he married in 2003.

She was born on October 7, 1979 in Rocky River, Ohio, and went to Rocky River High School.

Tracy later proceeded to DePauw University where she bagged a double degree major in conflict studies and political science.

She also played women’s football while in college.

Tracy went on to earn a law degree in Case Western Reserve University School of Law, and began her career journey as an attorney with Bingham Greenebaum Doll LLP.

Tracy and Brad met in high school and were together for many years.

The couple tied the knots in August 2003 and have been blessed with two children, Brandy and Kinsley.

Who Is Brad Stevens?

Brad Stevens is a former basketball coach and current president of basketball operations for the Boston Celtics.

The three-time Academic All-America nominee switched to coaching and volunteered for Butler University’s basketball team before the 2000–01 season.

From 2014 through 2021, Stevens guided the Celtics to every NBA playoff appearance, helped the team win a division title, and made three appearances in the Eastern Conference Finals.

In 2021, Stevens was appointed the Celtics’ president of basketball operations.
