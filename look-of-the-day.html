Kate Middleton Wore a Purple Suit With a Black Turtleneck

August 21, 2024
blog

The unsung hero of summer fashion is definitely those blister BandAids that accumulate at the bottom of our bags after countless emergency visits to CVS wearing fabulous yet spicy strappy heels. But, as our beloved sandals migrate south for the season and more sensible closed-toed shoes take their place, it's time to give some shine to another underrated wardrobe workhorse: patron saint of cool-weather layering, the turtleneck.

Kate Middleton, giving credit where credit is due, wasn't afraid to put the turtleneck to the task just as fall's page flipped. On a visit to Ulster University this week, Middleton wore a purple Emilia Wickstead suit, Emmy London Josie pumps, a Jaeger Kate Bag, Liv Thurlwell earrings, and a plain black turtleneck. The resulting look was fun, focused, and totally put together.

The thing about a turtleneck is that it goes with almost everything imaginable. Seriously, go ahead and imagine it: a suit, shorts, layered under a maxi dress, a pleated skirt, leather pants, truly and honestly whatever. This super simple garment — which starts at just $10 on Amazon — probably does more than any other piece in your closet.
