Vanderpump Rules pregnant Brittany Cartwright celebrates baby shower with costars after slamming

September 10, 2024
blog

PREGNANT Brittany Cartwright has celebrated her baby shower alongside her Vanderpump Rules costars.

It comes days after the mom-to-be clapped back at cruel trolls who taunted her about her weight, telling them to “rot in hell”.

Looking unfazed by the recent comments, Brittany, 32, kept things casual as she celebrated her upcoming birth with her friends.

She wore a long pastel tie-dyed top that reached down past her thighs, and carried a white dress that she looked to be changing into.

The wife of TV personality Jax Taylor donned a face mask with a floral design on it as she protected herself and her unborn baby during the pandemic.

Jax was spotted loading up the car with gifts from their friends at the end of the party – with Brittany looking in good sprits.

Also in attendance were fellow stars including Lala Kent, Kristen Doute and Kate Maloney – who was hosting at her house.

Earlier this week, she lashed out on Instagram for those criticizing her body, reminding them she is pregnant.

“If you are a person who comments on a pregnant woman’s body in a negative way, then you, my friend, can rawt in hailll,” she wrote, before adding: “Respectfully.”

She famously coined the catchphrase on Vanderpump Rules in 2017, when she learned Jax had cheated on her with costar Faith Stowers.

In November, Jax said on fellow mom-to-be’s Lala Kent podcast Give Them Lala that he tries to “reassure” his wife, who is insecure with her pregnancy body.

He explained: “What I think is extremely important and what I’m going through now with Brittany is just reassuring Brittany how beautiful she is.”

Jax went on: “She’s already beautiful, she’s already gorgeous, but I always see Brittany looking at herself and I know in her head she’s just thinking sometimes I think that she’s not pretty.

“… I do it anyway, but it’s super important for new dads, I think, just to make sure to let your wife know how beautiful she is at all times.”

That month, Brittany also defended the size of her baby bump writing on social media: “I am happy and healthy and I’m going to enjoy my pregnancy no matter what the trolls say.”

Earlier this month, Brittany and costar Lala Kent, who is also pregnant, hit the beach together.

The expectant moms showed off their huge baby bumps wearing bikinis and swimming in the ocean.

When they were ready to cover-up, Brittany wore a black dress and Lala slipped on a white robe.
