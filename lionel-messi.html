Lionel Messi Wiki

Lionel Messi is a 36-year-old footballer who was born on June 24, 1987. His zodiac sign is Cancer, and he hails from Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. Lionel Andrés Messi is his full name, and he is also known as Leo Messi. His father’s name is Jorge Messi, and his mother’s name is Celia Cuccittini. Matias, Rodrigo, and Maria Sol are his three siblings. Messi is the youngest of the four siblings.

Messi has Italian and Spanish ancestors on his father’s side. His great-grandparents came from Catalonia and the northwestern Adriatic Marche area of Italy. Messi also has largely Italian lineage on his mother’s side.

He began playing football with his brothers, Matias and Rodrigo Biancucchi, as well as his cousins, Maximiliano and Emanuel Biancucchi, from a young age. His cousins are both professional football players. Messi joined the local team Grandoli when he was four years old. This footballer was tutored by his father there.
