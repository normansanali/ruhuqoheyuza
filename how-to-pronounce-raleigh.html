How do you pronounce Raleigh?

Depends. If it’s related to the US, Such as the Capital of North Carolina, it’s pronounced Raw-Lee. But if it relates to something from the UK, such as the surname of Sir Walter Raleigh or the Raleigh Bicycle Company it would be pronounced Rah-Lee.

Pronounce Names.

Pronunciation:Rah-Lay
Origin:Old English

How do you say Raleigh bikes?

When you are talking about Sir Walter Raleigh, you pronounce it Rah-lee, not Rally. But when you are talking about Raleigh bike company, you say Rally not Rah-lee.
