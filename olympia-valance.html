Olympia Valance is an Australian actress and model known for her role in the soap opera Neighbours. She initially started her career as a model and made her first appearance on a TV Show on June 2, 2014, as Paige Smith in Neighbours. She has served as the brand ambassador of various popular brands. Her superior acting skills landed her the nominations for Best Daytime Star at the Inside Soap Awards and the Most Popular New Talent at the Logie Awards in 2015.
