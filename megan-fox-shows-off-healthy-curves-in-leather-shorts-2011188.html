Megan Fox Shows Off Healthy Curves in Leather Shorts

August 15, 2024
blog

Foxy figure!

Megan Fox flaunted her newfound shapely figure Wednesday in L.A. Wearing high waisted leather shorts and a low-cut tank-top, the 25-year-old actress showed off her healthy curves on the set of This is Forty.

In March, Fox's trainer Harley Pasternak opened up about his worries over the actress' weight. "Believe it or not she has this really fast metabolism," he told Hollywoodlife.com. "So her training is all about body toning and sculpting. With her it's really about she's eating enough of the right things. People thought 'Oh she must be anorexic,' but she's far from that!" 

Pasternak said the Jennifer's Body star struggles to gain weight. "She gets frustrated at times because she's like 'I need to put on weight.' So she actually weight-trains to keep on that lean muscle tissue," he said.

Back in 2008, she had to pack on the pounds for a movie role. "I'd lost a lot of weight and got really scrawny but I was told I had to put on a size for Transformers because Michael [Bay, the director] doesn't like skinny girls," she told Fox News' Pop Tarts.
