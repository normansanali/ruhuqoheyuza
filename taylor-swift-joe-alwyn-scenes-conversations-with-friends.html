What Taylor Swift Really Thinks Of Joe Alwyn's Romance Scenes

September 06, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn have been dating for years now, even fueling rumors that the two are engaged or even secretly married. Their relationship has been extremely private compared to all of Taylor's previous ones. Recently, the two have been a little more open about their relationship and discussing each other during interviews. With Joe Alwyn starring in a new Hulu show, titled Conversations with Friends, it became a topic about how Taylor Swift really feels about all of Alwyn's steamy scenes with co-star Alison Oliver.

Taylor Swift And Joe Alwyn's Relationship History

Many fans think Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn met at the Met Gala in 2016, when Swift was dating Calvin Harris. Since they started dating the same year they've both done a great job keeping things out of the public eye regardless of how famous Swift is. They've certainly succeeded in making their relationship a topic of speculation for fans who want to know more.

After Swift's album Reputation, she released Lover, Folklore, and Evermore. These albums gave fans some insight into Swift and Alwyn's relationship. The albums were all packed with songs about falling in love. This made it pretty clear to fans that their relationship was a strong one.

After years of being together and being spotted only a couple of times in public together, fans really thought they were engaged, planning a secret wedding, or even already married. The rumors were even more fueled when it was rumored that there were plans for an upcoming private wedding ceremony in 2020. It seems those rumors aren't really true but are not 100% confirmed to be false.

In an interview just last month, Alwyn commented on the engagement/marriage rumors. “I mean, the truth is, if the answer was yes, I wouldn’t say, and if the answer was no, I wouldn’t say.” Alwyn said in an interview. Fans still don't know, and it is unclear if they ever will really know. What they know for certain is that the two are a strong couple, and they're both happy together.

Joe Alwyn's Role In 'Conversations With Friends'

Conversations with Friends is a new Hulu television series based on the 2017 debut novel written by Sally Rooney. The novel follows two different couples who become involved with each other. The show premiered this month with Joe Alwyn as one of the main roles, Nick Conway.

When the trailer was released it was obvious that the role entailed Joe Alwyn in romance scenes with co-star Alison Oliver. Naturally, this sparked fans' curiosities about how Taylor Swift feels about her long-term boyfriend in a role like that.

Because the two are so private with their relationship it was also a shock when they both commented on it. Joe stated that although his character on the show becomes involved in an open relationship, he is "happy in a monogamous relationship” within his off-screen life.

His co-star, Alison Oliver commented on Swift's reactions to the scenes as well. She said Taylor was "totally cool" with it, and she is aware it is "all part of Joe's job." Although Taylor did not comment directly about the romance scenes, she did make a comment about the show overall.

As Swift usually does, she made a subtle Instagram story about her support of the show. She wrote, "Can confirm it's phenomenal." So it is pretty clear from all angles that Taylor Swift feels proud of the role Joe plays and is supportive of the show.

What's Next For Taylor Swift And Joe Alwyn's Relationship?

Taylor Swift is always working on something and fans know she can totally surprise them as she did with Folklore. Back in 2020, she dropped an entire full-length album out of nowhere. Since then, she has been working on her re-recorded albums. Fearless (Taylor's Version) and Red (Taylor's Version) are already out and fans are pretty sure her 2014 album,1989 will be her next re-recorded one. Swift also has a song titled, Carolina, appearing in the upcoming film Where The Crawdads Sing.

Just last week, Swift released the song This Love (Taylor's Version) on all streaming platforms after it appeared in a trailer for an Amazon Prime movie, The Summer I Turned Pretty. Swift also released Wildest Dreams (Taylor's Version) last year after it went viral on TikTok. Both songs being from her fourth studio album, 1989, makes it pretty clear to fans that it just may be the next re-recorded album.

Joe Alwyn has been in many acting roles, most notably, The Favourite in 2018. His role in Conversations with Friends is his third acting role on a television show. An upcoming movie titled, The Stars at Noon is a romantic thriller that Joe Alwyn will star in. The film will have its world premiere on May 25th, 2022 at the Cannes Film Festival. Both Swift and Alwyn have a lot going on within their careers and it is clear they are in a happy relationship that fans are getting further glimpses into.
