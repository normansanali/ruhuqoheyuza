A Striker (Japanese: サッカーせんしゅ Soccer Player) is a Trainer class introduced in Generation V. They are players of association football, known as soccer in most of North America, as well as Japan. They wear a sports jersey, shorts, long patterned socks, and cleats. They use soccer balls that are colored similarly to a standard Poké Ball.

They are found in the Big Stadium in Nimbasa City on Tuesdays and Fridays.
