Agatha Raisin Season 5: Agatha Raisin is a British comedy-drama television series based on the novels by M.C. Beaton, starring Ashley Jensen as the titular character, a former PR executive who becomes an amateur sleuth in the Cotswolds. The series premiered on Sky One in 2014 with a pilot episode, followed by three more seasons commissioned by Acorn TV, a streaming service that specializes in British and international shows.

The fourth season aired in 2022, featuring four feature-length episodes adapted from the books Agatha Raisin and the Deadly Dance, Agatha Raisin and the Love from Hell, Agatha Raisin and a Spoonful of Poison, and Agatha Raisin: There Goes the Bride. Plot and Storyline

The storyline of Agatha Raisin Season 5 has not been revealed yet, but we can make some predictions based on the books that the show’s producer hinted at adapting. The books are Hiss and Hers, The Busy Body, and Something Borrowed, Someone Dead, which are the 23rd, 21st, and 24th books in the series, respectively. The show does not follow the chronological order of the books, so it may change some details and events to fit the continuity of the series. Here is a brief summary of each book and how it may be adapted for the screen:

  • Hiss and Hers: Agatha develops a crush on a handsome gardener named George Selby, who is hired by many women in the village. However, her hopes are dashed when George is bitten by a snake and dies. Agatha suspects foul play and decides to investigate, with the help of James and Charles. Along the way, she uncovers George’s dark secrets and faces a deadly enemy. This book may introduce George as Agatha’s new love interest, who may or may not survive the season. It may also feature some snake-related scenes and a confrontation with a killer.
  • The Busy Body: Agatha is hired by the Parish Council to organize a health fair in the village. However, things go wrong when the star attraction, a quack doctor named Dr. Toynbee, is found dead in his hotel room. Agatha teams up with Toni and Roy to find out who killed him and why. Meanwhile, Agatha has to deal with the arrival of Harry, who wants to rekindle their relationship, and the interference of Sheila, who tries to sabotage Agatha’s investigation. This book may bring back Harry and Sheila as Agatha’s romantic and professional rivals, respectively. It may also feature some scenes of Agatha and her friends trying out various health products and services, and a mystery involving a cult and a conspiracy.
  • Something Borrowed, Someone Dead: Agatha is hired by a woman named Gloria French, who wants to find out who is poisoning her jam. However, before Agatha can start her investigation, Gloria is found dead in her cottage, apparently strangled by a scarf. Agatha discovers that Gloria was not well-liked by the villagers, as she had a habit of borrowing things and not returning them.
  • Agatha has to find out who hated Gloria enough to kill her, and what secrets she was hiding. Meanwhile, Agatha has to deal with the jealousy of Toni, who feels threatened by Agatha’s relationship with George. This book may continue Agatha’s romance with George, and explore Toni’s character development. It may also feature some scenes of Agatha and her friends making and tasting jam, and a mystery involving a missing child and a hidden treasure.

Recap

Season 4 of Agatha Raisin consisted of three episodes, each based on a different book by M.C. Beaton. The titles and synopses of the episodes are as follows:

  • Agatha Raisin and the Love, Lies and Liquor: Agatha goes on a seaside holiday with James, but things go wrong when she becomes the main suspect in the murder of a loud and obnoxious woman, who was killed with Agatha’s scarf. Agatha has to clear her name, while also dealing with James’s cold and distant behavior, and the secrets of the seaside town.
  • Agatha Raisin and the Spoonful of Poison: Agatha attends a jam-making festival, where she is asked to judge the entries. However, she soon discovers that someone has spiked one of the jams with poison, killing one of the contestants. Agatha has to find out who is behind the deadly prank, while also coping with the arrival of a new curate, who has a crush on her.
  • Agatha Raisin and There Goes the Bride: Agatha reluctantly agrees to attend James’s wedding to his Welsh fiancée, Eirwen, but things take a tragic turn when Eirwen is shot dead the night before the wedding. Agatha has to take on the case to clear James’s name, while also dealing with her own feelings for him, and the secrets of Eirwen’s family.
