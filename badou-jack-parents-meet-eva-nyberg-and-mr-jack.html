Badou Jack parents ethnicity

Jack’s father hails from the Gambia and his mother is a Swede.

The boxing legend competed for the Gambia as an amateur boxer at the 2008 Olympic games held in Beijing.

His boxing career which took off at the age of 18, has been super illustrious. He has managed to set a record of 150 wins and a paltry 25 losses in the light heavyweight division.
