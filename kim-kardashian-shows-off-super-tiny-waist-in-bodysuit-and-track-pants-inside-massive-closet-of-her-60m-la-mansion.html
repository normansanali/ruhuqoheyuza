Kim Kardashian shows off super tiny waist in bodysuit and track pants inside massive closet of her $

August 02, 2024

KIM Kardashian showed off her shrinking frame in skintight loungewear in her $60million LA mansion.

The reality star recently hit back at critics of her drastic 12-lbs weight loss, insisting she has dropped the weight in a “healthy way”,

And Kim ignored the backlash once again on Thursday as she displayed her tiny waist and legs in new pieces from her SKIMS line.

The mother-of-four wore a skintight gray bodysuit and shorts, before changing into a pair of slouchy track pants.

She showed off her figure in the clothes while filming herself in the huge closet of her Hidden Hills pad.

It comes after Kim addressed the backlash to her recent admission that she dropped 16lbs in three weeks to fit into Marilyn Monroe’s iconic dress for the Met Gala.

“If I was starving and doing it really unhealthy, I would say that, of course, that’s not a good message,” the Hulu star told Allure.

“But I had a nutritionist, I had a trainer. I have never drunk more water in my life.

“I don’t see the criticism for other people when they lost weight for roles — they are [considered] geniuses for their craft.”

The Hulu star added: “There are so many things out there that are so not accurate and not true.”

Back in May, Kim revealed the measures she took to drop so much weight.

The mother-of-four told Vogue at the Met Gala: “I tried [the dress] on and it didn’t fit me. I said, ‘Give me three weeks.’

WEIGHT LOSS

“I had to lose 16 pounds down today. It was such a challenge. It was like a [movie] role. I was determined to fit [into] it. I haven’t had carbs or sugar in about three weeks.”

And late last month, Kim admitted she has lost even more weight after sticking to the ultra-strict diet.

She said on The TODAY show: “It actually taught me a lot about my lifestyle and my health, and since then, afterwards, I continued to eat really healthy.

“I mean, I’m down 21 pounds now. I’m not trying to lose any more weight, but I have more energy than ever.”

FEARS FOR KIM

Meanwhile, The Sun exclusively revealed that Kim’s family is concerned she’s losing too much weight and is “barely sleeping”.

She has admitted her packed schedule of filming the family’s reality show, studying to become a lawyer, looking after her four children and her bi-coastal romance with Saturday Night Live star Pete Davidson has taken its toll.

“Kim looks amazing, always, but she’s good at keeping how stressed she is hidden,” an insider tells The Sun. 

“She’s the skinniest she’s ever been and barely sleeping. Her family knows that a lot of this is her rebounding from a very controlling marriage, and her determination to show Kanye how much better she is doing without him.

“But whereas before she was incredibly healthy and well-rested, now she’s constantly jetlagged and she’s finding it hard to fit workouts in. I think Kim’s really struggling but won’t admit it. 

“She is having a great time with Pete but the travel is taking its toll and she’s barely sleeping. Everyone is worried she’s burnt-out.”
