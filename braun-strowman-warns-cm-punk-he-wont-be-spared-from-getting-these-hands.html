Braun Strowman Warns CM Punk He Wont Be Spared from Getting These Hands

July 30, 2024
blog

Braun Strowman has had quite the rollercoaster ride in the world of pro wrestling and has become a household name on WWE’s SmackDown, but his path has been paved with injuries. While he remains absent from WWE television, Strowman decided to send a warning to CM Punk.

CM Punk’s return at the Survivor Series Premium Live Event became a significant event in pro wrestling history for many reasons. He has since appeared on Monday Night RAW, Friday Night Smackdown and even NXT Deadline.

That said, it is apparent he isn’t on the best terms with Seth Rollins and also Drew McIntyre for some reason. While speaking to India Today, Braun Strowman was asked about CM Punk’s alleged “quarrels” with Rollins and McIntyre. Strowman stated that he had no idea about that but made it clear that Punk won’t be spared from ‘getting these hands.’

“I’ve never met the guy personally, so I don’t know what his quarrels are with Seth Rollins, Drew McIntyre. I’ll make an assessment of what kind of person he is when I get to meet him, but just know he can get these hands like anybody else.” 

Braun Strowman also has a huge opponent in mind for his WWE return. We will have to wait and see if Strowman and CM Punk will ever have a feud in the future, as it might be an interesting choice.
