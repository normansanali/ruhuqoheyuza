In another sports star meets pop princess romance tale, NFl star Travis Kelce is dating iconic hitmaker Taylor Swift, and it looks as though their relationship is getting serious…

Taylor was pictured inside of the Kansas City Chiefs tight end's home where she enjoyed a pre-match lunch with his friends and family – how sweet!

Former NFL star Bernie Kosar shared the picture which revealed the cosy set-up, where Taylor gave a smile and thumbs up to the camera posing next to him.

"Watching the Browns game with @taylorswift13 pre KC game. Tough to see Deshaun Hurt!!!" he captioned the image, which he shared to X (formerly known as Twitter).

The snap was taken in a spacious open-plan room with marble-clad kitchen island and a seating area with cosy beige couch. There is a television mounted on the wall – perfect for viewing the pre-match build up!
