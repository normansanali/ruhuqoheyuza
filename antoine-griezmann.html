Antoine Griezmann is a French professional footballer who is best known for his career as a forward for La Liga club Atlético Madrid and the France national team. In the past, he has also been a part of teams like Real Sociedad and Barcelona. During the course of his career, he has won tournaments such as Segunda División, Copa del Rey, Supercopa de España, UEFA Super Cup, UEFA Europa League, UEFA European Under-19 Championship, UEFA Nations League, FIFA World Cup, and has been the recipient of honors such as La Liga‘s “Best Player”, La Liga‘s “Favorite player for the fans”, Trophées UNFP for “Best French Player playing Abroad”, UEFA European Championship‘s “Player of the Tournament”, “French Player of the Year”, and many more.
