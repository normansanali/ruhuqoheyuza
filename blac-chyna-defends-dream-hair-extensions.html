Blac Chyna Defends Daughter Dreams Hair Extensions

August 07, 2024
blog

Unbothered! Blac Chyna is not worried about people’s opinions on how she chooses to style her baby daughter’s hair.

The model received major backlash after she shared an Instagram photo of her smiling 17-month-old daughter, Dream, wearing pink hair extensions on Wednesday, April 25. Chyna captioned the now-deleted post: “My beautiful baby.”

TMZ caught up with her at LAX on Friday, April 27, and asked her about the controversy. “They’re called baby clips. Every little girl has them,” she said while standing next to her boyfriend, YBN Almighty Jay.

In regards to people’s reactions, she said: “I don’t care! Not at all.”

When the cameraman asked why people found it so offensive, she said: “Listen, I don’t care, my guy.”

Fans wrote in the comments section of her post on Wednesday. One user wrote: “It’s not necessary and it’s a baby. Not a doll to play to play with.” Others defended her saying that they are just clip-ons.

She later addressed the situation, which was caught on video, on social media. “Being famous is hard enough dealing with scrutiny but when someone feels comfortable to come and touch your child it’s a whole other story,” she wrote in a post on her Instagram Story. “I do not condone violence nor am I a violent person but shout out to all of the amazing mothers out there that will protect their children at all cost. Love, King and Dreams mommy.”
