Why Didn't They Ask Evans star Will Poulter's love life revealed

September 12, 2024

The actor portrays Bobby Jones in the Agatha Christie adaptation

    Will Poulter has returned to our screens in the Agatha Christie adaptation, Why Didn't They Ask Evans? In his role as Bobby Jones, the 30-year-old shares plenty of heartwarming scenes with his on-screen love interest, Lucy Boynton. But in real life, is the actor in a relationship? Here's what we know about Will Poulter's love life. 

    Is Will Poulter in a relationship?

    When it comes to his relationships, the TV star has always been notoriously private – but in August 2022, he was spotted kissing his model girlfriend, Bobby T. The couple couldn't have looked happier as they strolled down Sunset Boulevard holding hands. While they haven't been photographed together since, Bobby still follows Will on Instagram, so it looks like there's still a connection there.

    Will Poulter's dating history

    Prior to meeting Bobby, Will dated actress Yasmeen Scott from 2018 to 2019. They were photographed together at a number of events, including the Wimbledon Tennis Championship, the European premiere of Will's film , and a Milan Fall 2019 Menswear event, before calling it quits.

    In 2022, it was rumoured that Will had been dating his co-star, Florence Pugh, after they were pictured on a group holiday to Ibiza. Taking to Instagram, the actress quickly denied the reports, however. 

    "No, Will Poulter and I are not dating," she wrote. "We went to the beach with our friends, who are always about half a metre away from us in every picture, but have been cleverly cut out/ framed out so that it looks otherwise. 

    "You can LITERALLY see my best friend in the corner of so many shots and Archie's arms at the sides. I understand that the nature of this job is that you sometimes get your privacy completely bulldozed by paparazzi, but to fabricate this stuff actually does more damage than good." She added: "Thanks for saying we look sexy.. doesn't mean we're doing the sexy."
