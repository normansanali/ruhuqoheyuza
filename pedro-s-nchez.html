Biography

Pedro Sánchez was born in Madrid, Spain on February 29, 1972. Politician who is best known for serving as the Secretary-General of the Spanish Socialist Workers’ Party (PSOE). He was appointed to the position in 2014. On February 2, 2016, he was named Spain’s Prime Minister to form a government coalition following the 2015 elections. He served as the leader of the opposition in Spain while Mariano Rajoy served as the country’s Prime Minister. He graduated from Complutense University in 1995 with a dual degree in economics and business sciences. He first joined the PSOE political party in 1993 following Felipe Gonzalez‘s election win.
