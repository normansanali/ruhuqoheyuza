Has Bruce Springsteen Had Plastic Surgery?

As of this day, no official confirmation has been made about Bruce Springsteen going through plastic surgery.

Netizens are speculating about his plastic surgery on his facial area, as he looks much younger even though he is above 70 years old.

This news about him having plastic surgery started hovering on the web a few years back but no official confirmation about it had come from the side of the singer.

Similarly, this theory of plastic surgery came forward when a few internet fans researched and started comparing his pictures of past and present days.

According to the claim of the fans, Bruce has done his Hair Transplants, had received a Botox injection, and also had facial fillers around his face.

But all these are nothing but the hypotheses from of a particular person and no official confirmation regarding this matter is out in the public.
