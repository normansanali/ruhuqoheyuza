Giancarlo Purch is an American social media personality and an entrepreneur behind the Blazendary brand and the accompanying YouTube channel. Giancarlo Purch is best known for his sneaker-related content and fashion vlogs.

Career

Giancarlo launched his channel Blazendary on December 29, 2014. In the ensuing years, he has shared several videos related to high/low-end fashion and streetwear. Therefore, from videos on footwear and clothing collections to expensive shopping sprees, there’s everything on his channel for those who love fashion.

Some of his videos also depict fashion trends and are indeed very helpful for people who want to stay updated with the current style statements. Apart from fashion content, Purch’s channel also has several challenge-based videos, all of which are amazing. Additionally, there are a lot of collaborative videos on the channel that the YouTuber has made with his family and friends.

Purch also runs an enterprise under this name to sell his merchandise including apparel, accessories, and home goods. Irrespective of whether you are looking for a classy tee or a trendy iPhone case, Purch’s brand has got a lot to offer you. Moreover, one can shop for his products from the website shopblazendary.com.

He also has a second channel on YouTube. This gaming channel titled ‘Blazendary Gaming’ was launched on June 12, 2015. Although the channel has just two videos on it, it has still successfully earned over 7k subscribers. Furthermore, he opened his ‘Blazendary Pokemon’ YouTube channel on Jan 7, 2020.
