Ross Malinger Actor

Ross began his acting career as a child actor in the 1990s after starring in the movie Kindergarten Co. Malinger co-starred with Jean-Claude Van Damme in the 1995 film Sudden Death.

Ross provided the original voice of T.J. Detweiler on the animated series Recess. He did not reprise the role in the next seasons, as he was replaced by Andrew Lawrence. He was a recurring character on the teen drama Party of Five.
